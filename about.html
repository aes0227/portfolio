About Me

Hi! I am Alessandra Esquivel,


a current student at Northwestern University studying journalism, integrated marketing communications, and design. Since I was a kid, I’ve had a passion for photography and sharing how I see the world through my photos. My creative interests have led me to marketing and design, where I’ve been able to develop designs that visually draw people in.

I most value my versatility as it has allowed me to explore various career paths in marketing and design as well as helped me in adapting to new environments. Besides photography and graphic design, I love exploring new places and meeting people. I hope to continue traveling the world with my camera and share stories!
